Vulpem Notary

Blockchain-based notary service for indisputable time chain of distributed database changes.

Read more

Bitcoin based

At the time of writing timestamping on the Bitcoin blockchain is supported, the format is flexible enough to support a variety of methods.

Scalable

Thanks to batching and distributed calendar architecture, we can stamp and notorize petabytes of data and embed it on Bitcoin

Tamper-proof

A timestamp proves that some data existed prior to some point in time. Vulpem Notary defines a set of operations for creating provable timestamps and later independently verifying them.

Benefits

  • Forensic Auditability
  • Strong Attribution
  • Legal validity
  • Proof of existence
